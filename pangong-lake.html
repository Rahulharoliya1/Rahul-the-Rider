Pangong Lake: Travel Tips & My Story

Pangong Lake was one of the most peaceful and beautiful places I've ever visited. The turquoise blue water surrounded by mountains was surreal.

Getting there was tough — bumpy roads, altitude sickness, and freezing nights — but it was all worth it.

Tip: Start early, carry warm clothes, and stay for the sunrise — it's magical.
