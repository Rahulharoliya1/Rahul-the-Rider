Top 5 Things to Pack for a Bike Trip

Packing right can make or break your bike trip. Here are the top 5 essentials you must carry:

  1. Protective Gear: Helmet, riding jacket, gloves, and knee guards.
  2. Tool Kit & Spares: Basic repair tools, spare tubes, clutch wires.
  3. Navigation: Offline maps, phone mount, and power bank.
  4. Clothing: Weather-appropriate clothes, thermals, rain gear.
  5. First Aid & Essentials: First aid kit, medicines, sunscreen, and water bottle.

Being prepared ensures safety and allows you to focus on the ride.
