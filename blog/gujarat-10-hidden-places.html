Gujarat ke 10 Hidden Places (2025)

Gujarat ko log aksar Gir National Park, Rann of Kutch aur Somnath jaise popular tourist destinations ke liye jaante hain. Lekin Gujarat ka asli charm un jagahon me chhupa hai jahan abhi bhi bheed nahi pahunchi hai. Ye blog un bike travelers, solo explorers aur offbeat travel lovers ke liye hai jo popular routes se hatkar kuch naya dekhna chahte hain.

Maine khud Gujarat ke kai aise routes aur destinations explore kiye hain jahaan roads khud kahani sunati hain – jungle ke beech se guzarti sadkein, khali pahadi mod, aur shaant jagah jahan sirf hawa aur engine ki awaaz hoti hai.

1. Polo Forest (Sabarkantha)

Polo Forest Gujarat ke sabse underrated jungle destinations me se ek hai. Yahan ke curvy forest roads bike riders ke liye dream route jaise lagte hain. Ancient Jain temples, seasonal river aur green surroundings ise perfect weekend escape banate hain. Monsoon aur winter yahan ka best time hota hai.

2. Wilson Hills (Valsad)

Wilson Hills Gujarat ka eklauta hill station hai jahan se Arabian Sea ka view milta hai. Sunrise aur sunset ke time yahan ka scene cinematic lagta hai. Bike trip ke liye roads smooth hain aur traffic bahut kam hota hai, jo long peaceful ride ke liye perfect hai.

3. Dhinodhar Hill (Kutch)

Dhinodhar Hill spiritual aur adventure ka perfect mix hai. Yahan ka trek challenging hai aur upar pahunchkar jo silence milta hai wo city life ke noise ko bhula deta hai. Solo travelers aur meditation lovers ke liye ye jagah special hai.

4. Zarwani Waterfall (Narmada)

Statue of Unity ke paas hone ke bawajood Zarwani Waterfall abhi bhi offbeat mana jata hai. Jungle ke beech se guzarne wali road aur monsoon me waterfall ka powerful flow bike travelers ke liye thrilling experience deta hai.

5. Ninai Waterfall (Dang)

Dang district nature lovers ke liye hidden treasure hai. Ninai Waterfall ke aas-paas dense greenery aur tribal villages milte hain. Yahan ka environment slow travel ke liye perfect hai, jahaan aap sirf ride aur nature enjoy kar sakte ho.

6. Jambughoda Wildlife Sanctuary

Ye jagah eco-tourism aur jungle stay ke liye famous hai. Subah ke time jungle drive aur shaam ko shaant lake views travelers ko mental peace dete hain. Bike trip ke liye yahan ka route kaafi scenic hai.

7. Taranga Hills

Taranga Hills Jain temples aur rocky landscape ke liye jaane jate hain. Subah ke time fog ke beech temple view ek alag hi vibe deta hai. History aur adventure dono pasand karne walon ke liye ye jagah best hai.

8. Khambhalida Caves

Khambhalida Caves Gujarat ke hidden Buddhist caves hain. Yahan kam tourists aate hain isliye environment kaafi peaceful hota hai. Photography aur history lovers ke liye ye jagah perfect hai.

9. Hathni Waterfall

Monsoon ke time Hathni Waterfall apne full power me hota hai. Short trek aur thandi hawa ke saath ye destination weekend bike trip ke liye ideal hai.

10. Nakhatrana Grasslands

Winter season me ye grasslands bird watchers aur photographers ke liye heaven hain. Open landscapes aur empty roads long rides ke liye perfect lagte hain. Sunset ke time yahan ka sky unforgettable hota hai.

Conclusion

Gujarat sirf popular tourist spots tak limited nahi hai. Agar aap thoda alag route choose karte ho, to Gujarat aapko adventure, peace aur raw travel experience deta hai. Bike travelers ke liye ye state ek hidden gem se kam nahi.

Agar aap bhi bheed se door travel karna chahte ho, to ye hidden places aapki 2025 bucket list me honi hi chahiye.
