Gujarat ke 10 Hidden Places (2025)

Gujarat ko log aksar Gir National Park, Rann of Kutch aur Somnath jaise mashhoor tourist places ke liye jaante hain. Lekin inke alawa bhi Gujarat mein kai hidden aur offbeat destinations hain jo abhi bhi bheed se door hain.

Agar aap bike traveler, solo explorer ya nature lover ho, to yeh blog aapke liye perfect hai.

1. Polo Forest (Sabarkantha)

Jain temples, jungle roads aur peaceful environment ke liye famous.

2. Wilson Hills (Valsad)

Gujarat ka ek matra hill station jahan se Arabian Sea dikhta hai.

3. Dhinodhar Hill (Kutch)

Spiritual trek aur panoramic views ke liye best.

4. Zarwani Waterfall (Narmada)

Statue of Unity ke paas hone ke bawajood abhi bhi offbeat.

5. Ninai Waterfall (Dang)

Green surroundings aur nature escape.

6. Jambughoda Wildlife Sanctuary

Eco-tourism aur jungle drive experience.

7. Taranga Hills

Jain temples aur hilly landscape.

8. Khambhalida Caves

Ancient Buddhist caves with calm surroundings.

9. Hathni Waterfall

Monsoon me powerful flow aur scenic trek.

10. Nakhatrana Grasslands

Winter me bird watching aur photography ke liye perfect.

Conclusion

Agar aap Gujarat ko bheed se hatkar explore karna chahte ho, to yeh hidden places aapki bucket list me honi hi chahiye.
