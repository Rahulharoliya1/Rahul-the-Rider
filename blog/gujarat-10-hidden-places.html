Gujarat ke 10 Hidden Places (2025)

Gujarat ko log aksar Gir National Park, Rann of Kutch aur Somnath jaise mashhoor tourist places ke liye jaante hain. Lekin in popular destinations ke alawa bhi Gujarat mein kai aise hidden places hain jo abhi tak mainstream tourism se door hain.

Agar aap bike traveler, solo explorer ya nature lover ho, to yeh blog aapke liye perfect hai. Yahan hum Gujarat ke 10 offbeat aur kam-jaane jaane wale travel destinations ke baare mein baat karenge.

1. Polo Forest (Sabarkantha)

Polo Forest Gujarat–Rajasthan border ke paas sthit ek shant aur ghana jungle area hai. Yeh jagah Jain temples, nadi aur hilly terrain ke liye famous hai.

  • 10th–15th century ke Jain mandir
  • Monsoon aur winter mein best greenery
  • Bike riders aur campers ke liye ideal

2. Wilson Hills (Valsad)

Wilson Hills Gujarat ka ek matra hill station hai jahan se Arabian Sea dikhai deta hai. Abhi bhi yeh jagah kaafi had tak unexplored hai.

  • Sunrise & sunset viewpoints
  • Monsoon mein foggy roads
  • Shant environment

3. Dhinodhar Hill (Kutch)

Bhuj ke paas sthit Dhinodhar Hill ek spiritual aur adventurous destination hai. Yahan trekking aur panoramic views dono milte hain.

4. Zarwani Waterfall (Narmada)

Statue of Unity ke paas hone ke bawajood, Zarwani Waterfall abhi bhi ek hidden gem mana jata hai. Monsoon ke time yeh waterfall full flow mein hota hai.

5. Ninai Waterfall (Dang)

Dang district ka Ninai Waterfall nature lovers ke liye ek perfect getaway hai. Yahan ka green environment kaafi soothing hota hai.

6. Jambughoda Wildlife Sanctuary (Panchmahal)

Vadodara ke paas sthit yeh sanctuary jungle roads aur eco-tourism experience ke liye jana jata hai.

7. Taranga Hills (Mehsana)

Taranga Hills Jain temples aur offbeat hilly views ka unique combination deta hai. Photography ke liye bhi yeh jagah best hai.

8. Khambhalida Caves (Rajkot)

Khambhalida Caves Gujarat ke sabse purane Buddhist caves mein se ek hain. Yeh jagah history aur silence dono pasand karne walon ke liye perfect hai.

9. Hathni Waterfall (Pavagadh Area)

Hathni Mata Waterfall monsoon mein kaafi powerful flow ke saath dikhta hai. Short trek ke baad beautiful scenic view milta hai.

10. Nakhatrana Grasslands (Kutch)

Winter season mein Nakhatrana ke grasslands bird watching aur landscape photography ke liye kaafi famous hote hain.

Conclusion

Agar aap Gujarat ko bheed se alag nazariye se explore karna chahte ho, to yeh 10 hidden places aapki travel bucket list mein zaroor hone chahiye.

Bike travelers aur solo explorers ke liye Gujarat ke yeh offbeat destinations ek yaadgar experience dete hain.
