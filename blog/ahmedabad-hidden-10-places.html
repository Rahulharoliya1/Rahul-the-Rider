Ahmedabad ke Hidden 10 Places jo Aapko Zaroor Explore Karni Chahiye

Ahmedabad sirf Sabarmati Ashram, Kankaria Lake ya Atal Bridge tak hi limited nahi hai. Ye sheher apne andar kai aise hidden gems chhupaye baitha hai jo na to tourist brochures me milte hain aur na hi zyada logon ko unke baare me pata hota hai. Agar aap Ahmedabad ke local ho ya travel ke shaukeen ho, to ye blog aapke liye perfect hai.

1. Dada Harir Stepwell (Bai Harir ni Vav)

15vi sadi me bani ye stepwell Islamic aur Hindu architecture ka unique combination hai. Jaise-jaise aap neeche jaate ho, temperature naturally cool hota jata hai. Photography lovers ke liye ye jagah kisi treasure se kam nahi.

  • Shaant environment
  • Historic architecture
  • Best for photography

2. French Haveli (Old City)

Old Ahmedabad ki tang galiyon ke beech chhupi hui French Haveli ek heritage property hai jo French style aur Gujarati culture ka beautiful blend dikhati hai.

Best time: Morning ya sunset

3. Conflictorium – Museum of Conflict

Ye museum conflicts ko samjhane par based hai – personal, social aur global level par. Offbeat aur meaningful jagah pasand karne walon ke liye perfect hai.

4. Shreyas Folk Museum

Is open-air museum me Gujarat ke rural lifestyle aur folk art ko beautifully preserve kiya gaya hai.

5. Vaechaar Utensils Museum

Yahan 4500+ traditional bartano ka collection hai.

6. Malik Saban Tank (Kankaria ke paas)

Shaant aur less crowded jagah.

7. Vintage Car Collection (Kathwada)

Car lovers ke liye hidden paradise.

8. Hutheesing Jain Temple ke aas-paas

Shaant heritage experience.

9. Chandola Lake ke Quiet Corners

Sunset ke time soothing view.

10. Old Pols of Ahmedabad

Real Ahmedabad ka experience.

Conclusion

Ahmedabad har baar kuch naya dikhata hai – bas nazar badalne ki zarurat hai.
