1. Dada Harir Stepwell (Bai Harir ni Vav)

15vi sadi me bani Dada Harir Stepwell Ahmedabad ki sabse underrated heritage sites me se ek hai. Jaise-jaise aap seedhiyan neeche utarte ho, temperature naturally cool hota jata hai. Is jagah par Islamic aur Hindu architecture ka rare combination dekhne milta hai.

  • Perfect For: Photography & reels

2. French Haveli (Old City)

Old Ahmedabad ki pols ke beech chhupi hui French Haveli ek restored heritage home hai. Yahan French lifestyle aur Gujarati culture ka perfect blend dikhta hai. Morning sunlight me ye jagah aur bhi magical lagti hai.

Tip: Heritage walk ke saath visit karein.

3. Conflictorium â€“ Museum of Conflict

Ye museum sirf dekhne ke liye nahi, balki sochne ke liye hai. Yahan personal, social aur global conflicts ko interactive way me dikhaya gaya hai. Agar aap meaningful aur offbeat experiences pasand karte ho, ye jagah perfect hai.

4. Shreyas Folk Museum

Shreyas Folk Museum Gujarat ke rural lifestyle ko preserve karta hai. Yahan folk art, traditional homes aur handicrafts dekhne milte hain. City ke shor se door ye jagah bahut shaant feel deti hai.

5. Vaechaar Utensils Museum

4500+ traditional bartano ka collection is museum ko unique banata hai. Ye museum Ahmedabad ke culture aur daily life ko alag nazariye se dikhata hai.

6. Malik Saban Tank

Kankaria ke paas hone ke bawajood ye jagah kaafi shaant rehti hai. Local log yahan evening walk ke liye aate hain.

7. Vintage Car Collection (Kathwada)

Car lovers ke liye ye jagah kisi hidden paradise se kam nahi. Yahan rare vintage cars ka collection dekhne milta hai.

8. Hutheesing Jain Temple ke Aas-Paas

Temple ke aas-paas ka area kaafi peaceful hai. Architecture lovers ke liye ye ek perfect stop hai.

9. Chandola Lake ke Quiet Corners

Sunset ke time Chandola Lake ke kuch corners bahut soothing lagte hain. Photography aur solitude dono ke liye achhi jagah hai.

10. Old Pols of Ahmedabad

Agar aap Ahmedabad ko sach me samajhna chahte ho, to Old Pols ki walk zaroor karo. Yahi sheher ki asli soul hai.

Conclusion

Ahmedabad sirf ek heritage city nahi hai, balki ek experience hai. Agar aap thoda hatkar explore karte ho, to ye sheher aapko hamesha kuch naya dikhata hai.
